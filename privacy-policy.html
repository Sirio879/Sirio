Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for SIRIO

Effective date: July 20, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how SIRIO and its desktop applications, including VIMS, Shoventory, and HRMS, handle information when you visit our website or use our software. SIRIO is designed around local-first operations, which means your business data is typically stored on your own device or in your own controlled environment unless you choose a different setup.

What information we may collect

  • Website information: basic technical details such as browser type, device information, and pages visited, which may be collected by the hosting environment and/or analytics tools.
  • Contact information: if you request a demo, download, or support assistance, we may receive the information you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, or company name.
  • Account credentials: when using the application, users may need to create an account, and the credentials are controlled by the SIRIO administrator for access and authentication purposes.
  • Application data: SIRIO applications are primarily designed to work with data that remains under your control. In most cases, that data is stored locally on your computer or in your own local network/database environment.

How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the SIRIO website and desktop applications.
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests.
  • Understand product usage and improve performance, reliability, and customer experience.
  • Maintain the security and integrity of our services.

Data storage and security

SIRIO is built to keep business data as close to the user as possible. By default, your data is expected to remain on your own machine or in your own controlled environment. We do not sell your data, and we do not use your business data for advertising purposes. When you use third-party services, integrations, or cloud-connected systems, those services are responsible for their own security practices and data handling.

Third-party services

Our website may use third-party services such as hosting providers, font/CDN services, and analytics tools. These services may collect standard technical information as part of their operation. Please review the privacy policies of those providers where applicable.

Your choices

Because much of the information you use with SIRIO is stored locally or within your own environment, you are generally able to manage or remove it directly through your device, software settings, or local storage systems. If you have a question about your personal information or want to request deletion or correction of any information you shared with us, please contact us using the contact options available on the SIRIO website.

Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how SIRIO handles information, please contact us through the support or contact information provided on the SIRIO website.
