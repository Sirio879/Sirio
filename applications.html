Data Ownership Policy

Your Data. Your Control.

  • All business data is stored locally on your computer.
  • SIRIO cannot access your invoices, inventory, employee records, or business information.
  • Only login credentials required for authentication are managed by the SIRIO Admin.
  • Users have complete ownership and responsibility for their business data.
  • We recommend regular local backups to ensure data safety.

SIRIO is designed with privacy, performance, and user control as its core principles, giving businesses a secure desktop solution without compromising ownership of their valuable information.
